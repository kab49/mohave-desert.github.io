New World Blackbirds

Icteridae

The icterids are a group of small to medium-sized, often colorful passerine birds restricted to the New World and include the grackles, New World blackbirds, and New World orioles. Most species have black as a predominant plumage color, often with yellow, orange, or red. Sixteen species have been recorded in Nevada.
